<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.2.0" tags={["improvement", "feature"]}>

## Packaging Enhancements

- Introduced eco-friendly, resealable pouches that extend shelf life by `>30%` while maintaining freshness.
- Added tamper-evident seals for enhanced safety and customer trust.
- Updated nutritional labels with clearer allergen information and QR codes linking to recipe ideas.

## Improvements

- Optimized portion sizes for single servings, reducing food waste.
- Enhanced print quality on packaging with vibrant Kerala-inspired artwork.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-09-10" description="v1.1.0" tags={["feature"]}>

## New Flavors

- Launched Malabar Biryani and Appam with Stew packs, capturing traditional Kerala spices.
- Added Coconut Curry Chicken and Fish Molee variants for seafood lovers.

## Bug Fixes

- Corrected minor spice balance issues in earlier batches reported by customers.
- Improved heat retention in microwaveable trays.

</Update>
