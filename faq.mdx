## Ingredients and Allergens

We source fresh ingredients directly from Kerala farms to ensure authenticity.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Where do you source your ingredients?" default-open="true">
    You receive spices, coconut, and vegetables from certified organic farms in Kerala. We prioritize local suppliers for freshness and sustainability.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="What allergens are in your products?">
    Common allergens include nuts (cashews in some curries), mustard, and sesame. Products like Malabar Fish Curry are free from gluten and dairy. Review the label or our allergen chart below.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
