## Pairing Suggestions

Enhance your Celebrations Kerala ready-to-eat dishes with complementary sides. These ethnic Kerala flavors, like spicy fish curry or coconut-rich vegetable stew, pair perfectly with traditional accompaniments.

| Dish                  | Recommended Pairings                  | Why It Works                  |
|-----------------------|---------------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Kerala Fish Curry     | Steamed rice, pickle, fried poppadums | Balances spice with neutral rice |
| Vegetable Stew        | Appam or idiyappam, boiled eggs       | Creamy texture contrasts soft appam |
| Chicken Biryani       | Raita, onion salad, mango chutney     | Cools heat and adds freshness |
| Beef Fry              | Parotta, thoran (cabbage stir-fry)    | Crispy parotta soaks up rich gravy |

<Callout kind="tip">
Heat products gently on low flame to preserve authentic Kerala spices. Avoid microwaving for more than 2 minutes to prevent overcooking.
</Callout>

## Customizing Flavors

Tailor Celebrations Kerala products to your taste by adding fresh ingredients. Use these methods to adjust spice levels or introduce new twists.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mild Version" icon="sun">
    Reduce heat by adding a spoonful of coconut milk or yogurt. Stir in fresh curry leaves for aroma without extra spice.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Spicy Boost" icon="flame">
    Temper with extra mustard seeds, dried red chilies, and garlic. Finish with a squeeze of lime for tanginess.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Fusion Twist" icon="sparkles">
    Mix into pasta sauce or tacos. For Indo-Chinese, toss with stir-fried noodles and soy sauce.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Sample Recipe: Kerala Fish Curry Bowl

Transform your Celebrations Kerala Fish Curry into a complete meal in under 10 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Base" icon="fish">
    Heat the ready-to-eat fish curry pouch in a pan over medium heat for 3-5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Veggies" icon="carrot">
    Stir in diced carrots, green beans, and tomatoes. Simmer for 2 minutes until tender.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Serve" icon="utensils">
    Spoon over hot basmati rice. Garnish with coriander and serve with lemon wedges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Preservation and Shelf Life

<Expandable title="Storage Best Practices" default-open="true">
Store unopened pouches in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within 2 days. 

Shelf life:
- Unopened: 12 months from manufacturing date
- Opened (refrigerated): 48 hours
- Frozen: Up to 1 month (thaw overnight in fridge)

<Callout kind="alert">
Do not refreeze thawed products to maintain quality and safety.
</Callout>
</Expandable>
