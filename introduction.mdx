## Our Heritage

<Expandable title="Learn about Kerala's Culinary Roots" default-open="false">

Kerala's cuisine draws from Arab, Portuguese, and local influences, featuring coconut milk, curry leaves, and tamarind. Celebrations Kerala partners with family-run kitchens in Kochi to maintain authenticity.

Key traditions include:
- **Onam Sadhya**: Multi-course feasts on banana leaves.
- **Seafood Specialties**: Fresh catches from the backwaters.

This heritage ensures you taste history in every pouch.

## Quick Preparation Guide

Follow these steps to enjoy your meal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Heat" icon="fire">
    Remove pouch from outer pack. Microwave on high for 2-3 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Serve" icon="utensils">
    Tear pouch and pour into bowl. Garnish with curry leaves if desired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy" icon="heart">
    Pair with rice or appam. Serves 2.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  For best results, use a microwave-safe plate. Store unopened pouches in a cool, dry place.
</Callout>
