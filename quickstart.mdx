## Unboxing Your Celebrations Kerala Meal

Welcome to Celebrations Kerala, bringing the authentic ethnic taste of Kerala straight to your table with our ready-to-eat meals. Each pack contains premium ingredients capturing flavors like spicy chicken curry, fragrant biryani, or coconut-rich stews. Follow these steps to unbox, prepare, and savor your first meal.

<Callout kind="success">
Your Celebrations Kerala pack arrives sealed for freshness. Check the expiration date on the pouch before opening.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Unbox the Package" icon="package">
    Remove the outer carton and inspect the vacuum-sealed pouch. It stays fresh for `>6 months` unopened at room temperature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Store Properly" icon="fridge">
    Refrigerate at `4°C` or below after opening. Consume within `48 hours`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare Your Meal" icon="zap">
    Choose your heating method below. Your meal is ready in under `5 minutes`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Heating Instructions

Select your preferred method using the tabs below. All methods preserve the rich Kerala spices and aromas.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Microwave" icon="zap">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Pierce the Pouch">
        Make `3-4` small holes in the pouch top with a fork to release steam.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Heat">
        Place on a microwave-safe plate. Heat on high for `2-3 minutes` for a `300g` pouch. Stir halfway if needed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Serve">
        Carefully open the pouch (steam is hot). Transfer to a bowl.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Stovetop" icon="flame">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Transfer Contents">
        Cut open the pouch and empty into a saucepan.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Heat Gently">
        Add `2 tbsp` water. Heat on medium for `4-5 minutes`, stirring occasionally.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Temperature">
        Ensure it reaches `>75°C` internally for safety.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Safety and Handling

Always handle hot pouches with care to avoid burns. Do not consume if the seal is broken or damaged. Suitable for all ages, but consult a doctor for allergies to nuts, seafood, or dairy in specific variants.
